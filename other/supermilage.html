Supermilage club

Summary:

The Cal Poly Supermilage Club is a team of students who design and build a highly efficient vehicle to compete in the Shell Eco Marathon competition. John was a member of the Cal Poly Supermilage Club throughout his sophomore year at Cal Poly. He led the design and manufacturing of a new brake system for the vehicle.
