PG&E - Diablo Canyon Nuclear Power Plant

Summary:

John spent the summer of his junior year as an intern at PG&E's Diablo Canyon Nuclear Power Plant. He worked in the Outage Management Department, a team that deals with the refueling and maintenance of the power plant during planned shutdown periods. The internship program included weekly classes detailing various systems and departments within the power plant.
