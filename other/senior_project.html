Senior design project

Summary:

John’s senior design project was completed in 2019 over the last year of his Bachelor's degree at Cal Poly SLO. He worked with a team of three other mechanical engineering students to design an autonomous and waterless solar cell cleaning system. The project was sponsered by Fracsun Inc., a company specializing in PV soiling measurements.
